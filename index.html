How does it work?

Myagon provides adaptive training plans that use your previous workouts and future availability to determine the best workouts to do next so that you achieve your objective. The algorithm will take all your objective metrics such as heart rate, distance, pace, and other subjective metrics such as Rating of Perceiving Exertion (RPE) or feeling after workout, to select the next workout type and its key performance metrics (for example, average Heart Rate) to achieve.

Get your basics right!
The first objective is to identify your starting point, in order to bencharm your progress along the way. This is achieved by performing activities like interval runs, long runs, etc.
Select your distance
The program is aimed at getting you in your best shape for your “A” race, or your main race of the year. Other races will be taken into account to develop the plan.
Follow the plan
No training plan is a magic solution, you need to follow it and be accountable. By providing feedback on the sessions, the training plan will automatically adapt.
